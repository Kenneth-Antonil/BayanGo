Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

BayanGo values your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it to provide delivery services.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, mobile number, delivery address, order details, and app usage information needed to process and fulfill your requests.

How We Use Information

Your information is used to confirm orders, coordinate deliveries, improve service quality, and send transaction-related updates.

Data Sharing

We only share necessary information with riders, merchants, and service providers involved in completing your order and operating the app.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns, please contact BayanGo support through official app channels.
