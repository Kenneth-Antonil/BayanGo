About Us

BayanGo is a local delivery service built to make grocery shopping easier for Binangonan and Talim Island communities.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable, and convenient access to fresh market goods through a simple mobile-first ordering experience.

What We Do

We source products from Pritil and partner merchants, then coordinate delivery through organized AM and PM rider runs.

Our Commitment

We aim to support households with fair pricing, transparent updates, and dependable fulfillment from checkout to doorstep.
